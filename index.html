03 — Insights Key Findings

What the data actually says

Beyond the numbers — the themes that matter for product teams, marketing, and customer experience decisions.

Strength
Camera quality dominates praise
Camera, photography, and photo quality are the most mentioned terms in positive reviews. The 48MP system is a clear purchase driver.
Pain Point
Battery life is the #1 complaint
Battery, charging, and heating appear disproportionately in negative reviews. A recurring issue despite hardware upgrades.
Strength
Performance consistently praised
Fast, smooth, performance, and speed feature heavily in high-polarity reviews. The A16 chip is meeting expectations.
Pain Point
Price perception creates friction
Expensive, overpriced, and value-for-money appear frequently in negative reviews, especially from first-time Apple buyers.
Strength
Design & build quality wins
Build, design, premium, and look are strong positive signals. Customers associate physical quality with the Apple brand.
Pain Point
Heating issues flagged repeatedly
Heat and overheating appear in a cluster of negative reviews, often alongside gaming and extended usage scenarios.
